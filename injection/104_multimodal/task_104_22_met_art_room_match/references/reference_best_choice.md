# Reference Best Choice

Best final recommendation:

- `Two Men Contemplating the Moon`
- artist: `Caspar David Friedrich`
- object id: `438417`

Why this is the hidden best answer:

- the muted dusk palette fits the room's desaturated blue-gray wall color
- the quiet, contemplative mood directly matches the reading-nook brief
- the lower-contrast atmosphere works better in soft north-facing light than
  the brighter Monet or the more energetic van Gogh
- the horizontal composition suits the wall placement above a low bookshelf

Why the other two are weaker:

- `Garden at Sainte-Adresse`: too sunlit, bright, and airy for the requested
  dusk-like calm
- `Wheat Field with Cypresses`: too energetic, saturated, and brushwork-heavy
  for a quiet low-noise corner
